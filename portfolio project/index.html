' Dear Green Planet '

I never knew how beautiful life could be without you. My heart rhythms like a piano every time I see the wonders of your full green wonders . This feeling is never felt before, and it is inspiring, sweet and wonderful. When I wake up in the morning, I think about you and your glorious rich planet, I am in deep love. We will never be apart, while everyone else resides on planet mars, me and you will last forever. This connection between us is so string and only destiny can take us apart but our love will never fall short. Looking forward to to spend my life with you, beautiful Green Planet. May I be the person to bring joy and happiness, yes our unity truly is unlike anything else in this dimension, our message will reach across many universes for time indefinite. Sincerely, Rafael Philix
